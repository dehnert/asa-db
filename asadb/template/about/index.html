Help & About

The ASA Group Database is provided principally for the use of the ASA Executive Board, MIT student group officers, current and potential group members, and MIT offices supporting those groups.

To contact the Association of Student Activities, please see our contact information. For issues directly about the ASA Group Database, please contact the database team at asa-db@mit.edu. Make sure to include your group's name, and preferably a link to your group's database page. If you're following up on an email from us or the database, please make sure to quote the original message.

See also the following pages on the ASA website:

  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Information on visibility of account numbers
  • Use of Data

The database was originally developed by Alex Dehnert. Continuing maintenance has been by the ASA Executive Board, in particular Geoffrey Thomas and Rachel Meyer.
