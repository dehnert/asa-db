Membership Confirmation

One of the requirements of new groups is that they consist of at least five active members. You must have five MIT students sign an electronic form attesting that they will become active members of the group after it receives ASA recognition. Please refer your members to https://asa.scripts.mit.edu:444/applications/startup/confirm-membership.py to have them sign.

Anti-Hazing Form

Before this group becomes an official group, you must have an officer of the group submit an anti-hazing form, once again using your certificates to sign. All ASA groups must complete this once per year, so don't be surprised when you get another notice about this next year.
